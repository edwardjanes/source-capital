# Source Capital — Brand Guidelines
> For use by LLMs and contractors when producing any visual or written content for Source Capital.

---

## 1. Brand Overview

**Company:** Source Capital  
**Website:** sourcecapital.co.uk  
**Positioning:** Investment advisory and consultancy for startup founders raising $250k–$1M. Dubai-based, globally operating.  
**Brand character:** Authoritative, precise, and high-performance — the brand signals access, credibility, and results. Not warm or approachable. Not flashy. Elite but direct.

---

## 2. Colour Palette

### Primary Colours

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| Brand Green | Electric Green | `#00FF84` | Primary accent. Headlines, CTAs, logo wordmark "SOURCE", key highlights, dividers, buttons |
| Background | Pitch Black | `#000000` | Default background for all dark assets |
| Text/Logo | Off-White | `#E8E8E8` / `#F0F0F0` | Body text, "CAPITAL" wordmark, secondary text on dark backgrounds |

### Secondary / Supporting Colours

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| Dark Surface | Near-Black | `#0D0D0D` or `#111111` | Card backgrounds, section panels on dark layouts |
| Muted Text | Light Grey | `#A0A0A0` | Captions, metadata, supporting copy |
| White | Pure White | `#FFFFFF` | Use sparingly — for maximum contrast moments only |

### Colour Rules
- **Never** use the brand green as a background for large sections — it is an accent only.
- **Never** use warm tones (orange, yellow, red) unless explicitly approved.
- **Never** place green text on a white or light background — it reads poorly and breaks the dark-first identity.
- All default layouts are **dark-first** (black or near-black background).
- A light/white layout is permitted for document formats (PDFs, proposals) but must retain green and off-white as the accent system.

## 3. Typography

### Typefaces

| Role | Font | Weight | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| Headlines / Display | Inter | ExtraBlack (900) | ALL CAPS. Used for major headings, hero statements, product names |
| Subheadings | Inter | Bold (700) | Sentence case or ALL CAPS depending on context |
| Body Copy | Montserrat | Regular (400) | All running text, bullet points, descriptions |
| Supporting Text | Montserrat | SemiBold (600) | Labels, callouts, table headers |

### Type Rules
- Headlines are **always uppercase** when set in Inter ExtraBlack.
- Never mix fonts outside this system. Do not substitute with system fonts (Arial, Helvetica, etc.) unless explicitly noted as a fallback.
- Minimum body text size: **14px / 10.5pt** for digital; **10pt** for print.
- Line height for body: **1.5–1.6×** the font size.
- Letter spacing on display headlines: **0.02–0.05em** (slightly tracked out for impact).
- **Fallback stack (if Inter/Montserrat unavailable):** `'Inter', 'Montserrat', sans-serif`

## 4. Logo

### Versions

| Version | Description | When to Use |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Full Colour on Black | "SOURCE" in `#00FF84`, "CAPITAL" in off-white, black background | Primary — use whenever possible |
| Mono White on Black | Full wordmark in off-white/white, black background | When green cannot be reproduced (single-colour print) |
| Full wordmark | "SOURCECAPITAL" set as one block, two-tone | Standard lockup for most assets |

### Logo Rules
- **Never** place the logo on a light or white background without explicit approval. The logo is designed for dark surfaces.
- **Never** recolour "SOURCE" to anything other than `#00FF84` or white (mono version).
- **Never** use a drop shadow, glow, or outline on the logo.
- **Never** stretch, compress, or alter the letterform proportions.
- **Never** recreate the logo in a different typeface — the custom letterforms (notably the "E" with horizontal bars) are part of the identity.
- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the "S" character on all four sides.
- Minimum reproduction size: **120px wide** digital / **30mm wide** print.

### Logo Signature Detail
The "E" in "SOURCE" and "CAPITAL" uses a custom treatment with **two horizontal bars** (similar to a stylised equals sign), not a standard three-bar E. This must be preserved in all representations.

## 5. Imagery & Visual Style

### Photography
- **Style:** Dark, high-contrast, editorial. Think boardrooms, city skylines, financial data, founder-investor meetings.
- **Tone:** Aspirational but grounded. No stock-photo clichés (handshakes, fake smiles, generic office shots).
- **Colour treatment:** Dark or desaturated backgrounds preferred. Green tint overlays are permitted as a brand accent.
- **Avoid:** Brightly lit lifestyle imagery, pastel tones, casual consumer aesthetics.

### Graphic Style
- Geometric and minimal. No decorative flourishes, drop shadows, or gradients unless they serve a clear purpose.
- Dividers and rule lines should be in `#00FF84` or a 1–2px off-white line.
- Section highlight blocks (like the "RAISE TEAM / DATA ROOM" card style) use a near-black fill (`#111111`) with a green left border or header.
- Icon style: line icons or filled minimal icons — monochrome, matching text colour.

### Layout Principles
- **Dark-first.** All social, advertising, and presentation assets default to black backgrounds.
- **Generous whitespace.** Do not crowd the layout.
- **Left-aligned body copy.** Centre-align headlines only when the design calls for it (hero statements, full-width banners).
- **Green is a signal.** Use it to draw the eye — CTAs, key stats, product names, separators. One or two green elements per composition maximum.

## 6. Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes
- **Direct** — says what it means, no padding.
- **Authoritative** — speaks from expertise, not aspiration.
- **Outcome-focused** — frames everything around results, not process.
- **Confident, not arrogant** — assured, but never condescending to founders.

### Tone Matrix

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Headlines / CTAs | Bold, punchy, no fluff | "Close Your Seed Round in 12 Weeks." |
| Product descriptions | Clear, benefit-led, no jargon | "A full data room, built for you — pitch deck to financial model." |
| Outreach / DMs | Warm, direct, no pressure | "Always around when you need me — just shout." |
| Social content | Expert POV, real talk | "Most founders pitch the wrong investors. Here's why." |
| Proposals / documents | Professional, precise, evidence-backed | No waffle, lead with the outcome. |

### Writing Rules
- British English throughout. ("Optimised" not "optimized", "colour" not "color", "cheque" not "check".)
- Avoid corporate filler: "synergy", "leverage" (as a verb), "holistic", "ecosystem" (used loosely), "innovative".
- Numbers in headlines: use numerals ("12 Weeks", not "Twelve Weeks").
- Use em dashes (—) for emphasis breaks, not hyphens.
- Sentences should be short and active where possible.

## 7. Product Names

Always write product names exactly as below. No abbreviations unless contextually obvious.

| Product | Format | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Raise Launchpad | Title Case | Entry-level Skool community, $97/month |
| Raise Accelerator | Title Case | 90-day done-with-you programme |
| Raise Velocity | Title Case | Premium done-for-you raise service |

- "Raise" is always capitalised as part of a product name.
- Do not prefix with "the" unless in natural sentence flow ("the Raise Accelerator programme").

---

## 8. Prohibited Uses

- ❌ Green as a large background fill
- ❌ Logo on light/white backgrounds
- ❌ Gradients applied to the logo
- ❌ Warm colour accents (orange, yellow, red)
- ❌ Rounded, bubbly, or playful design elements
- ❌ American English spelling in written copy
- ❌ Generic stock photography
- ❌ Comic Sans, Papyrus, or any decorative or script typeface
- ❌ Stretching or distorting the logo
- ❌ Recreating the "E" letterform in standard fonts
