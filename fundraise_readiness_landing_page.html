The problem

Most founders don't know why investors say no

They assume it's the market, the timing, or bad luck. Usually, it's something fixable — and they went in before fixing it.

🎯

They're pitching the wrong investors

Targeting funds that don't match their stage, cheque size, or sector — and wondering why they're not getting callbacks.

📊

Their deck doesn't do the job

Missing slides, weak narrative, or an unclear "why us" — the deck answers none of the questions investors are actually asking.

📈

Their traction story is thin

Either too early, or they have traction but can't communicate what's driving it — which raises more questions than it answers.

💬

They can't pitch in under 3 minutes

No clear verbal pitch. They ramble into product detail before investors understand the opportunity. First impressions don't recover.
